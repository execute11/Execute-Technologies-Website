Digital Transformation Roadmap for African SMEs

Comprehensive guide to implementing effective digital strategies
Published: April 12, 2025
Author: Daniel Osei
Format: PDF (2.4 MB)
Reading time: 15 minutes

This comprehensive guide provides practical steps for small and medium enterprises across Africa to leverage technology for business growth without overwhelming their resources or capabilities.

Based on our experience with over 50 SME digital transformations, we've developed a framework that addresses the unique challenges of the African business context, including connectivity constraints, skills availability, and regulatory considerations.
