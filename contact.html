Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about working with Execute Technologies

What is the typical timeline for a custom software project?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but our typical custom software development process ranges from 3-6 months for mid-sized projects. We provide detailed timeline estimates during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Our development approach includes structured sprints with regular milestone reviews, ensuring you have visibility throughout the process and can see tangible progress.

How do you handle projects for businesses outside of South Africa?

We work with clients across Africa through a combination of virtual meetings and periodic on-site visits for critical project phases. Our distributed collaboration methodology enables effective delivery regardless of geographic location.

We utilize secure cloud-based collaboration tools, maintain regular communication schedules that accommodate different time zones, and establish clear governance frameworks to ensure seamless project management across borders.

What industries do you primarily serve?

We have extensive experience across financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, agriculture, education, retail, and government sectors. Our sector-specific expertise allows us to understand the unique challenges and opportunities in each industry.

Each industry team includes consultants with relevant domain experience who understand the regulatory environment, operational workflows, and key performance indicators specific to your sector.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we provide comprehensive post-implementation support through our managed services offerings. Our service level agreements can be tailored to your specific needs, from basic technical support to full 24/7 system monitoring and management.

Our support packages include regular maintenance, security updates, performance optimization, and user training to ensure your solution continues to deliver value long after the initial implementation.

How do your solutions address Africa's unique connectivity challenges?

Our solutions are specifically designed with Africa's connectivity realities in mind. We implement offline-first architectures, progressive enhancement, and data synchronization strategies that allow systems to function effectively even in limited connectivity environments.

We also optimize for low bandwidth consumption, implement efficient caching mechanisms, and design interfaces that load quickly on a variety of devices, including older mobile phones that are common across the continent.

What payment terms do you offer for your services?

We offer flexible payment structures based on project size and duration. Typically, this includes a milestone-based payment schedule with clear deliverables tied to each payment. For longer engagements, we can establish monthly retainer arrangements.

We accept multiple payment methods and currencies to accommodate businesses across different African markets. For startups and SMEs, we also offer scaled engagement models that align with growth stages and budgetary constraints.
