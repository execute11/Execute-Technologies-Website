Cloud Migration Accelerates Growth

Savanna Financial Services

Challenge

Savanna Financial Services, a leading financial services provider in South Africa, struggled with aging infrastructure that limited scalability and created rising maintenance costs. With operations across three countries, they needed a solution that addressed varying regulatory requirements while improving performance.

Solution

Execute Technologies delivered a comprehensive cloud migration strategy that:

  • Assessed 47 applications for migration suitability
  • Developed a phased transition plan minimizing disruption
  • Implemented a multi-cloud architecture addressing regional compliance requirements
  • Created automated scaling capabilities responding to demand fluctuations
